Types of Leaves

Serrated leaves

  • Toothed leafs are a common type of leaf.
  • They are defined by their jagged edges and oval like features.
  • Learn to identify them by observing there "sharp" edges.

Lobed leaves

  • Lobed leaves are another common type of leaf.
  • They are able to be identified by there slightly three pronged "clover" like shape.
  • They are most often times found on Oak and Sweetgum trees.
  • They have deep indented margins.

Smooth leaves

  • Smooth leaves are yet another common type of leaf.
  • They are defined by their smooth edges that are nice to the touch.
  • There margins have give or take wavy sinuous structure.
